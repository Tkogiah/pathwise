# Pathwise Strategic Direction (Summary)

## Positioning

Pathwise is evolving from a workflow prototype into a **workflow-first nonprofit case management platform** focused on housing, human-services, and regulated case-management environments.

The core promise is to replace wall-of-text casework with **visible roadmaps, task state, ownership, blockers, appointments, and progress views** across manager, worker, and trainee contexts.

## Market Focus

Near-term fit:
- Nonprofit housing and human-services organizations
- Case-management-heavy teams
- VSP-adjacent workflows where privacy and access controls are critical

## Compliance Direction (Wording Guidance)

- Use **HIPAA** (not “HIPPA”).
- Do **not** claim “HIPAA certified.” There is no official HHS certification.
- Do **not** claim “HUD certified” or “HMIS certified.” HUD does not certify specific products.

Preferred phrasing:
- **Designed to support HIPAA-ready deployment patterns**
- **Aligned to HMIS data standards / comparable database requirements**
- **Working toward HMIS-comparable readiness**

## Product Direction

Pathwise is not trying to replace the system of record on day one. It is a **workflow visibility and execution layer** that surfaces structured state, due dates, and blockers in a way that improves accountability and handoffs.

## Packaging (Long-Term)

- **Pathwise Core** — roadmap engine + task/state model + notes + appointments + basic reporting
- **Compliance Core** — RBAC, audit logs, retention/deletion, security controls
- **Housing / VSP Edition** — HMIS-comparable workflows, reporting/export alignment, privacy constraints

## Roadmap (High-Level)

- Phase 1: compliance-ready foundation (RBAC, audit logging, retention policy, security model)
- Phase 2: pilot readiness (templates, manager reporting, onboarding, exports)
- Phase 3: HMIS comparable gap closure (field coverage, reporting validation)
- Phase 4: HIPAA-ready hardening (if required by buyers)
